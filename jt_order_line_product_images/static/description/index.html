Order Line Product Images

Organized view with product images in order lines view for Sales, Purchase, Invoicing, Manufacturing and Inventory

By default odoo not provides order lines images for sales, purchase, invoicing, manufacturing and inventory. So jupical comes with new feature to display product image in order lines.

This module has been tested in Community and Enterprise version 12

After installation, Go to Sales here you see order lines with product images.




Then, Go to Purchase and here you see purchase order lines with product images.




Then, Go to Invoicing and here you see invoice order lines with product images.




Then, Go to Manufacturing and here you see Manufactoring Order's lines with product images.




Then, Go to Manufacturing and here you see Bill of Material lines with product images.




Then, Go to Inventory and here you see inventory order lines with product images.
